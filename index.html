My Work

Foodlicious an online food ordering system

Foodlicious is an online food ordering system that allows customers to order a wide range of foods directly from the website. The system was developed and implemented using React, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB. It features user and admin dashboards for efficient management of users, products, and orders. Customers can track their orders in real-time and receive updates. The system ensures secure user authentication, integrates with Stripe for seamless payment processing, and offers a user-friendly food ordering experience.

Presented solutions and results in a comprehensive report, emphasizing the potential for further optimization and improved efficiency of the system to enhance road safety.
